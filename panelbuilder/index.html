Comic Viewer Panel Builder

  1. Drag your comic image file onto the workspace below to begin.
  2. Use the Panel tool to drag across the dimensions of the first panel.
  3. Use the Selector tool to tweak your panel, adjusting its size and position.
  4. Repeat the process until you have created all your panels.
  5. Your finished panel data is now available to copy and use with the comic viewer.
